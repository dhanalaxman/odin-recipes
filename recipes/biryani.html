Biryani Recipe

Description

Biryani is a fragrant, celebratory South Asian rice dish featuring layered long-grain basmati rice, marinated meat (chicken, lamb, fish) or vegetables, and rich spices. It is characterized by its distinct, layerd prepartion where rice and meat are cooked separtely before being combined and steamed together-a technique known as dum-often sealed with dough to infuse flavors.
